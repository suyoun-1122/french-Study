# Yeonjae French V4 build plan 1. **Build 1 — Foundation**: project structure, design tokens, responsive and accessibility base. 2. **Build 2 — Home**: rebuild the home dashboard to match the approved pastel game mockup. 3. **Build 3 — Characters**: apply the approved Petit, Fromage and Lavande character artwork and guide states. 4. **Build 4 — Quiz**: compact quiz screen, guaranteed-answer logic, feedback and reward effects. 5. **Build 5 — Ingredients**: learning rewards, inventory and persistence. 6. **Build 6 — Kitchen**: recipe requirements, cooking interaction and completion states. 7. **Build 7 — Collection**: French food collection, gender labels and cultural descriptions. 8. **Build 8 — Final QA**: mobile optimization, animations, content validation and release packaging. The fixed product loop is: learn → quiz → earn ingredients → cook → collect French dishes.
